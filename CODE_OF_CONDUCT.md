# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, intimidation, or threats.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code.
